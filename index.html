Trusted by Nashville Favorites

For 10 years, Mojo Cookie Dough & Creamery has helped make events across Nashville even sweeter. Known as one of Nashville’s Best Ice Cream Trucks, Mojo delivers a high-energy dessert experience that guests remember long after the event ends.


From corporate gatherings and school celebrations to university events, medical teams, hospitality groups, and luxury brands, Mojo has earned a reputation as one of Nashville’s Most Popular Ice Cream Trucks—bringing premium treats and unforgettable service to every occasion.


We’re proud to serve organizations throughout Nashville and the surrounding area with premium ice cream, freshly baked cookies, signature floats, and made-to-order treats from our vibrant truck.
